WriteWithMe What is it?

What

WriteWithMe is your digital writing partner. She has a somewhat abstract writing style, and loves creative writing. If at times you find it hard to see the connection between what she writes and what you previously had written, please rest assured that it is all part of the grand scheme of things that she has planned for your collective story.

How

WriteWithMe gets to write quite a bit on a day to day basis, so she lets you start and set the theme. Once you have written your first part, she will think for a while, and then write a bit herself. Afterwards you can write some more, and then, again she will contribute a part. At the end you will have a well written and entertaining piece of prose. Have fun.
